Digitalization projects

My Repair

MyRepair is a custom application built with AppSheet designed to digitize the repair dossier processing and tracking system for the repair stations at Saint-Éloi and SATAIR plants.

During my apprenticeship, I was responsible for designing, developing, deploying, and continuously improving this application, which has since become central to repair dossier management at the Saint-Éloi facility.

MyRepair has:

· Reduced time spent by operators and planners on dossier tracking.

· Centralized repair information for streamlined workflows.

· Automated KPI calculations via an integrated dashboard, enhancing data-driven decision-making.

Load and capacity headcount tool

This project aimed to automate the data flow between operational requirements and human resource availability for a large-scale industrial department St Eloi plant (Methods & Maintenance).

· Data Engineering: Developed a custom data cleansing script (Apps Script) to process unstructured legacy sources and transform them into a structured relational database.

· Architecture & Integration: Established a dynamic link between collaborative workload estimation tools (Google Sheets), HR resource tracking (FTE), and historical forecast data.

· Visualization & BI: Designed and deployed an interactive Looker Studio dashboard to monitor workload coverage and team distribution in real-time.

· Value Proposition: Early identification of workload peaks (overload) and troughs (underload) to optimize labor costs.
