Finance projects

My Budget

Historically, the financial tracking provided by the Saint-Eloi finance department lacked expenditure forecasts. This forced departments to maintain their own manual, decentralized "shadow" trackers to manage future spending. Consequently, each team used independent calculation methods, leading to significant time wasted on data reconciliation and frequent misalignments with financial controllers. Project leaders lacked a single, cross-functional tool to monitor both Actuals and Forecasts at the plant scale.

I developed a Qlik Sense solution to automate and centralize the monitoring of Operating Expenses (Opex NRC). In order bridge the gap between official financial data and operational forecasts.

· Data Integration: Merges diverse streams including budgets, forecasts, actuals, purchase orders (Click & Buy), and granular employee clocking data.

· Granular Insights: Features advanced filtering by team, Aircraft Program, F-Line, ect.

· Impact: Created a "Single Source of Truth," drastically reducing the time spent on manual reporting and ensuring perfect alignment between operations and finance.

S&P500 Dashboard

I developed a data pipeline and interactive dashboard to analyze the globally recognized S&P 500 index. The dashboard updates to the current date and provides multiple analytical views with tailored indicators:

· Overview: Macro-level analysis of the index’s global movements.

· Sector View: In-depth breakdown of the index by sector composition and inter-sector dynamics.

· Individual Stock: Technical analysis of specific S&P 500 companies, including advanced indicators.

· Portfolio Simulator: Allows users to simulate a custom portfolio of S&P 500 stocks (sourced from a modifiable Excel sheet) and track hypothetical performance.
